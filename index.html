BECOME A BETTER ATHLETE TODAY

Here at EliteFeet we focus on our abilities enroute to becoming better and better each day, the ELITEFEET way. Our mission is to teach proper footwork so the athlete is granted with speed, agility, and quickness that can take their game to the next level.

The reason we focus so much on this is because it does 3 essential things:

Prevents Injury by improving body positioning that will significantly decrease the loads that are places on parts of the body at risky angles.

Helps with Balance by allowing the athlete to control their center of gravity. This aids in the athletes stability, change of direction, initiation of movement and grants balance throughout the range of movement.

Lastly it is the Most Effective Key to Movement. Since the feet are the only parts of the body in contact with the ground it gives them better ability to absorb the shocks and Ultimately get to the ball in the right place at the right time.
